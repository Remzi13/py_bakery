Introduction

Bakery Manager is a lightweight web application designed to help small bakeries track their raw materials, product recipes, sales, and expenses. By integrating recipes with stock management, the app automatically calculates material usage when sales are recorded.

Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a bird's-eye view of your bakery's performance today and highlights items that need attention.

  • Total Sales Today: Shows the total revenue generated from all sales recorded since 00:00.
  • Low Stock Items: Alerts you to ingredients or materials that have fallen below a safe threshold.

Products & Recipes

This is where you define what you sell and how it's made.

Adding a Product

  1. Go to the Products tab.
  2. Click Add Product.
  3. Enter a Name and Sale Price.
  4. Select ingredients from the list and specify the quantity used for one unit of this product.
  5. Click Save.

Pro Tip When you sell a product, the system automatically subtracts the required ingredients from your stock according to the recipe.

Stock & Ingredients

Manage your raw materials and packaging here.

Ingredients

Before adding products, define your ingredients in the Ingredients tab. Specify the unit of measurement (kg, g, l, pcs).

Stock Management

The Stock tab tracks the current physical quantity of your items. You can manually adjust stock here if needed, but primary consumption happens via Sales.

Sales Tracking

Every time a customer buys something, register it in the Sales tab.

  • Discounts: You can apply a percentage discount at the moment of sale. The total price will adjust automatically.
  • Stock Impact: As mentioned, recording a sale triggers a check on the product's recipe and reduces ingredient quantities.

Expenses & Suppliers

Track where your money is going beyond just raw materials.

  • Suppliers: Maintain a contact list of your vendors in the Suppliers tab.
  • Expenses: Record utility bills, rent, or large material purchases. Expenses can be linked to specific suppliers for better accounting.

Write-offs

Use the Write-offs tab to record waste, spoilage, or expired products.

  • Selecting Product write-off will reduce finished product stock (if tracked separately) or simply record the loss.
  • Selecting Stock Item will directly reduce the quantity of a specific ingredient (e.g., if a bag of flour was damaged).
